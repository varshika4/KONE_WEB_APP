Latest News and Stories

New Smart Elevators Transform Urban Landscapes

KONE has recently unveiled its new line of smart elevators, designed to enhance the efficiency of vertical transportation in modern cities. These state-of-the-art elevators come with advanced features such as predictive maintenance, real-time performance monitoring, and seamless integration with building management systems. The introduction of these smart elevators aims to address the growing demand for smarter and more efficient urban mobility solutions.

KONE Launches Eco-Friendly Elevator Models

In a significant step towards sustainability, KONE has launched its new range of eco-friendly elevator models. These elevators feature energy-efficient technologies and environmentally friendly materials, reducing their carbon footprint and contributing to greener buildings. The new models also incorporate regenerative drives that capture and reuse energy, further enhancing their environmental benefits.

KONE Wins Prestigious Design Award

KONE is proud to announce that it has won the prestigious Design Excellence Award for its innovative elevator design. This award recognizes the company's commitment to blending aesthetics with functionality, creating elevators that not only perform exceptionally well but also enhance the visual appeal of modern architecture. The award-winning design features sleek lines, customizable finishes, and user-friendly interfaces that set a new standard in elevator design.
